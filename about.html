About CareerPath

CareerPath is an innovative platform designed to help school students identify their strengths, interests, and potential career paths through scientifically crafted psychometric and activity-based assessments.

  • Target Audience: Students from 6th to 12th standard
  • Languages: Available in English and Tamil
  • Mission: Empowering students to make informed career choices
